Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Wonderfully rich chocolate frozen yogurt recipe. The taste is like chocolate cream pie filling with a bit of tang from the yogurt. It has lots of calcium but is low in fat and calories. A sweet treat you can feel good about!

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons cornstarch
  • 1 can fat-free evaporateed milk
  • 1/2 cup semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup plain lowfat yogurt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Combine sugar and cornstarch in a medium saucepan. Stir in evaporated milk and chocolate chips. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until chocolate is melted and mixture is thickened.
  2. Remove the saucepan from heat and stir in yogurt and vanilla. Refrigerate until chilled.
  3. Pour chilled mixture into an ice cream maker. Freeze according to the manufacturer's instructions.
